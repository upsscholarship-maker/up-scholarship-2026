UP Scholarship 2026-27 Highlights

  • Scholarship for Pre-Matric & Post-Matric students
  • Online application and renewal system
  • Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT)
  • Transparent verification process

Eligibility Criteria

Students must be permanent residents of Uttar Pradesh and enrolled in a recognized institution. Income limits and eligibility conditions differ by category and course.

Required Documents

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • Income Certificate
  • Bank Account Details
  • Previous Year Marksheet

How to Apply for UP Scholarship 2026-27

  1. Complete online registration
  2. Fill application form carefully
  3. Upload necessary documents
  4. Submit and save application receipt
